Lanja
House

Located in the rural farmlands on the outskirts of Ratnagiri, the brief for this project was to create an ancestral home that blends in with the tranquil surroundings of Lanja. The idea was to build a place that would revive childhood nostalgia. A crucial aspect of the project was that it had to be budget-friendly.
